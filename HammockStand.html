I Built a Hammock Stand

My friend wanted to build a hammock stand, so I was like sure I'll build a hammock stand.

So, he bought a bunch of 2" x 4" pieces, and I cut them to size at our University Makerspace. They were all pretty simple cuts and it was straightfoward to assemble, the hardest part was fitting it in the car and not getting ticketed for it sticking out of the trunk.

Completed Hammock Stand!
